hi, my name is zach miller. you've arrived at my personal space on the interweb. here you will find a collection of projects i've worked on, links exploring that work, and potentially links to other parts of the interweb with work by other people i find interesting and or important.

if you're wondering what it is i do for work, you might classify me as an musician, audio engineer, film editor, film director/producer, computer and tech enthusiast, or any combination of the above.

i've had people call me a systems thinker or general creative, but in all honesty, i just find myself attracted to all forms of video/audio art, the technical tools that facilitate those mediums, and how to best continue doing that work.

as a result of these interests, i'm currently exploring the realm of financial co-operatives and DAOs for a project that i'm working on called secret teaching.
